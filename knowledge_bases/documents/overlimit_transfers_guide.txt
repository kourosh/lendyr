Lendyr Bank SOP-CC-FT-042: Handling Over-Limit Funds Transfer Requests

This knowledge base provides guidance for customer service representatives handling over-limit transfer requests.

Standard Daily Transfer Limits:
- Internal transfers: $10,000
- ACH transfers: $5,000
- Domestic wires: $25,000
- International wires: $10,000

For transfers exceeding these limits, CSR escalation and supervisor override may be required.
Additional authentication and fraud screening procedures apply for high-value transfers.

Regulatory Compliance:
- BSA (Bank Secrecy Act)
- USA PATRIOT Act
- FinCEN Travel Rule
- OFAC screening
- Regulation E
- Regulation CC

Required Authentication:
- Step-up authentication for high-value transfers
- Caller-supplied number restrictions
- Mandatory fraud screening

Escalation Procedures:
1. Verify customer identity
2. Check transfer limits
3. Assess fraud risk
4. Obtain supervisor approval if needed
5. Document in CRM system
